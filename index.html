Rules & Info

For the 1-year platformer anniversary, we have decided to start a creator contest!

  • The theme will be Natural Disasters
  • You will have 4 weeks to build and submit your part into the website. The deadline is January 17 at 23:59 UTC
  • Colab entries are allowed (if you win with a collab entry you will have to split the money with the person(s) you worked with)
  • The difficulty of the level should be around hard demon to insane demon
  • The gamemode has to be Platformer
  • There is no restriction on length, but it still needs to have good gameplay, decoration, and atmosphere throughout
  • One entry per person or collab
  • NONG songs are allowed
  • If levels are deemed to not be natural disaster themed, they will not be judged
Rewards depending on placement are as follows:
  • #1 - $25 + Spot in a future mega collab
  • #2 - $20 + Spot in a future mega collab
  • #3 - $15 + Spot in a future mega collab
  • #4 - $10 + Spot in a future mega collab
  • #5 - $5 + Spot in a future mega collab
  • #6-#20 - Spot in a future mega collab
The staff of Pemonlist will be the ones judging the levels. We will be rating them in three different areas on a 10-point scale and then averaging these results into one score. The three different areas are as follows:
  • Gameplay
  • Decoration
  • Atmosphere

We wish you all good luck!
